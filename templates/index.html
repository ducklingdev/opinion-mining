Opinion Mining

Sentiment analysis a.k.a opinion mining, is an approach to natural language processing (NLP) that identifies the emotional tone behind a body of text. See Examples below:
text I:   My experience so far has been fantastic! Positive
text II:   The product is ok I guess Neutral
text III:   Your dev team is useless! Negative
